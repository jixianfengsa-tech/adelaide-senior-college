About Us

Enriching Lives Through Culture & Education

A not-for-profit community organisation serving Adelaide's Chinese-speaking senior community through educational programs, cultural activities, and social connection.

🏫

Our Mission

Adelaide Chinese Senior College is dedicated to promoting active ageing within culturally and linguistically diverse communities. We provide accessible, high-quality educational and recreational programs that foster wellbeing, social inclusion, and cultural connection.

Our programs are designed to reduce social isolation, promote physical and mental health, and celebrate Chinese cultural heritage within the broader Australian community.
