About Us

Enriching lives through culture and education

Adelaide Chinese Senior College is a not-for-profit community organisation dedicated to promoting active ageing within culturally and linguistically diverse communities across Adelaide.

We provide accessible, high-quality educational and recreational programs that foster wellbeing, social inclusion, and cultural connection — helping seniors stay active, engaged, and connected.
