About Us

More than a college — a caring community

Adelaide Chinese Senior College is a registered Australian charity and community-based organisation dedicated to promoting healthy ageing, lifelong learning, wellbeing and social inclusion for culturally diverse seniors across South Australia.

Operated by SA Asian Senior Learning Centre Inc. (ABN 33 863 362 720), we have grown rapidly since our establishment in December 2024 to 383 registered members as of July 2026.

Our Mission

To promote healthy ageing, lifelong learning and social inclusion by providing educational, cultural, recreational and support services that empower seniors to remain active, connected and valued members of the community.

Our Vision

To build an inclusive and vibrant community where seniors continue to learn, contribute, connect and thrive throughout later life.

Advocacy & Support Services

More than just classes

We support seniors beyond the classroom — helping navigate aged care, digital literacy, and wellbeing services. We are a genuine community service organisation.

🏥

My Aged Care Assistance

Help understanding and accessing My Aged Care services, including home care packages and residential care options.

🏠

Support at Home Guidance

Information and referrals for in-home support services — cleaning, meal delivery, personal care, and home modifications.

💻

Digital Literacy

One-on-one help with smartphones, computers, online banking, MyGov, and staying connected with family online.

🛡️

Elder Abuse Awareness

Information and referral pathways for seniors experiencing financial, emotional, or physical abuse. You are not alone.

🤝

Social Isolation Support

Programs designed to connect isolated seniors with community activities, peer support groups, and social outings.

📞

Information & Referral

Not sure where to turn? We can point you to the right government and community services for your situation.

⚖️

Justice of the Peace (JP) Services

Free JP services for seniors — document witnessing, statutory declarations, certified copies, and guidance on government forms. Available by appointment.
